Hello!

I’m Kirsten Carter and welcome to my portfolio website!

I am a recent graduate of Oregon State University’s Post-Baccalaureate Computer Science (2021) program entering into a Software Developer/Engineer career after spending 7 years in the tourism business in the Pacific Northwest. In my computer science degree, I took elective classes in networking, developing RESTful API's for use in Google Cloud Computing, and UX/UI. This culminated in a group capstone project where myself, Thomas Draxler, and Abraham Serrato Meza created a HTML5 tower defense game called Computer Defense Force built using the Phaser 3 game engine, JavaScript, Node.js, Express (backend), and the ES6 linter and GitHub.

My previous degree is a BA in Integrated Media with a minor in History from Pacific University (2013) where I studied multiple forms of digital media types including photography, graphic design, and website development, culminating in my capstone project where I created and inserted visual effects using Adobe After Effects, InDesign, and Photoshop for another student’s capstone film "Neon Black".

Here will be a central place to link my projects, both professional and hobby, for people to find.
